Olympiad

The longer you wait for future the shorter it will be.

Here at IATSAT we help students to compete with students on a similar educational level with clarity of concepts. It helps to identify a child's capability and real potential that will help him survive better in today's competitive world. Our Olympiads motivate students to endeavour for better and deeper understanding of scientific and rational facts so as to enhance their reasoning, analytical and problem solving skills.We have designed our Olympiad keeping a futuristic approach in mind that will also help the student to prepare themselves better for further competitive exams .

IATSAT believes in creating a community of lifelong learners, responsible global citizens, and champions of our own success

INDIVIDUAL SCHOOL

The IATSAT is divided into National and International Olympiad

  • Junior Olympiad (grade 3 to 9)
  • Young Mind Olympiad (grade10 to 12)

Different Levels of Olympiad

International level (Top five teams from National level will be participating at International level along with participants from sixty countries.)
  • State level (Students can register through their school OR our portal.)
  • Zonal level (Selected students from state level will be participating at zonal level.)
  • National level (Selected students from zonal level will be participating at National level in a group.)
  • International level (Top five teams from National level will be participating at International level along with participants from sixty countries.)
