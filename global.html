IATSAT Global

IATSAT global is developed through careful research and analysis to cater to students who are looking to study abroad. Our tests are aimed at an international audience and not associated with any particular education system; thus, all candidates stand an equal chance. Personal Interview will also be conducted to evaluate the potential of the candidate.

The Higher education system across the globe isn’t at right pace not right place.
Major Problems:

  • Universities are facing problems through their channel partners.
  • The lack of mentorship financial hardship and academic issues.
  • The dropout is increasing day by day.
  • Leads to the skill shortage across the globe.
  • This is affecting the world economy indirectly.

After many years of research IATSAT finally came into existence. IATSAT is world’s first 100% philanthropy platform.

IATSAT is an assessment test conducted by “One Alphabet Educational Society’’ India & Australia in association with various Universities across the globe to provide a platform for intellectual and creative students who are willing to pursue their higher education overseas. IATSAT is developed through years of research & analysis of industry professional specially catering to the students looking for an education outside their home country. Since IATSAT Exam is fully recorded ,the surveillance video will be provided to the Universities or Colleges which will help in selection processof the best candidate,personal interview will also be conducted to evaluate the potential of the candidate.

About the exam

To ensure that IATSAT becomes the standard across the International lines, we have added several advantages for students appearing in the IATSAT Exam.

Test Format

  • Listening
  • Numeric
  • Reasoning
  • Verbal Ability
  • General knowledge
  • Life Skills

Eligibility Criteria

  • Diploma, 10+2(senior secondary) with aggregated, marks of 60%.
  • For Masters, graduation with aggregate of 60% with a recognized college or University.
  • Sabbatical of one year is accepted.
  • Medical students will not be eligible to appear in scholarship programme this year.
  • Students will be permitted (for IATSAT exam)up to twoattempts only .

WHY IATSAT?

IATSAT is 100 % academic and can be given from anywhere in the world through our authorized channel partner. It is fully computerized & you can get your result within a week.

Who Accept IATSAT Test?

Universities across the globe. areA accepting the IATSAT Test as their entrance test & we are continuously adding more academic partners.

Declaration Of IATSAT Score

We declare the result on the basis of performance of the candidate which includes:

  • 95% from IATSAT Exam
  • 5% from their academic performance.
